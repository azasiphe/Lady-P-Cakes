About Us

Welcome to Lady P'Cakes

My name is Palesa Duma, and I am the proud owner of Lady P'Cakes. My journey began, where I worked as a cleaner at a cake company in 2015. Over time, I learned the art of baking from the talented bakers I worked with, and my passion for creating beautiful cakes grew stronger.

After the COVID-19 pandemic reshaped the world, I decided to follow my dream and launch Lady P'Cakes, bringing joy to every celebration with custom cakes baked from the heart. Today, we specialize in cakes for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, and other special moments, made to make your event truly unforgettable.
